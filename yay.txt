Steps to Disable Speed Limiter on ACTBEST Apex E-Bike:

1. Power on the bike.
2. Hold "+" and "−" buttons together for 3–5 seconds to enter settings.
3. Navigate to setting "P08" using the "Power" or "Mode" button.
4. Increase "P08" value to "100" using "+" button (100 = no limit).
5. Press "Mode" again or wait to save and exit.
6. If locked, try passcodes: 0000, 1234, 6666, or 8888.

Note:
- No speed wire to cut – limiter is software-based.
- Use off-road only. Exceeding 28 mph may be illegal on public roads.
- Removing the limiter may void your warranty.
